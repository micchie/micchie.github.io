Michio Honda

Systems researcher at NEC Labs Europe

I will be joining School of Informatics at University of Edinburgh as Lecturer in January 2020
I do systems research in NEC Laboratories Europe at Heidelberg. I moved back here on November 2016 from Advanced Technology Group (ATG) of NetApp where I worked for two years in Munich. I finished my PhD in March 2012 at Keio University, Japan. I am broadly interested in networked systems. In 2008 I joined Nokia Research Center, Espoo, Finland as an intern, and in 2010 I joined University College London (UCL) as a visiting student researcher.

One of active projects is PASTE, co-designing network and storage stack for persistent memory systems.
